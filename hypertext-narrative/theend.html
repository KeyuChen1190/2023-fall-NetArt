The end

银烛秋光冷画屏，

轻罗小扇扑流萤。

天阶夜色凉如水，

卧看牵牛织女星。

The silver candlelight in autumn casts a cold glow on the painted screen, A light silk curtain flutters as fireflies dance around. The night sky at the heavenly stairs is cool like water, Lying down, I watch the stars of the Cowherd and Weaver Maid in the sky.
